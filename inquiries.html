Vault Inquiries

Why a vault?
Our one of a kind luxury vaults are meant to meet your every need for generations to come. We invested a lot into bringing all that we have to offer and our vaults are something we have come to be very proud of.
How are the vaults sustained?
Every one of our vaults runs off of nuclear power which is not only clean energy, but is sustainable for hundreds of years. Our vaults provide water purification as well as the capability to grow crops for increased sustainability.
What should I bring?
Be minimal about personal effects. Some items deemed unnecessary may be denied. There is limited space in each vault and a vault suit will be provided to each vault attendee as a form of regular attire within the vaults. And don't worry, we made sure they are extra comfortable!
